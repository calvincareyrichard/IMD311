Reminiscing the old days.

Sitting in my room, recalling the old days when I used to have good times in Campus, going out with my friends, listening to our lecturer's notes. Those are the good days. I remember we would come 1 hour early to the classes just to get the aircon, especially the evening classes. Since the MCO, I just have to do online classes at the home. To be honest, it is not as exciting anymore.
